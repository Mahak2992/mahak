Frequently Asked Questions

What is your turnaround time?

Turnaround time varies based on project complexity. For standard YouTube videos (10-15 minutes), I typically deliver within 3-5 business days. Short-form content like Reels can be completed in 1-2 days. For corporate projects or longer content, I'll provide a custom timeline during our consultation.

How do you handle revisions?

All projects include two rounds of revisions at no additional cost. This ensures we achieve your vision while maintaining project timelines. Additional revision rounds can be arranged at an hourly rate. I value clear communication throughout the process to minimize extensive revisions.

What file formats do you work with?

I work with most standard video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, and ProRes. For optimal quality, I recommend providing footage in the highest resolution available (preferably 4K or 1080p). I can deliver final videos in any format required for your specific platform or purpose.

How do we share files?

For file transfers, I use secure cloud services like Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer depending on file size and your preference. For ongoing projects or larger files, I can set up a dedicated shared folder for seamless collaboration.

What are your payment terms?

For new clients, I require a 50% deposit before starting work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion before final file delivery. For returning clients and ongoing partnerships, I offer flexible payment options including monthly retainers. I accept payments via bank transfer, PayPal, and major credit cards.
